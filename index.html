About

I design and build clinical software systems with a focus on data integrity, tenant isolation, and long-term operability.

I'm the founder and lead engineer behind Tembo EMR and Clinical Records Hub (CRH) — platforms built for African healthcare environments, handling de-identified patient data, secure document verification, and controlled data reuse for research.

I care more about systems surviving misuse and edge cases.

Systems & Architecture

Platform Architecture

  • Multi-tenant EMR with strict tenant isolation
  • Per-tenant Google Cloud Storage buckets (blast-radius containment)
  • BigQuery for analytics on de-identified clinical datasets
  • Asynchronous pipelines using Celery & Redis
  • Signed PDFs & QR-based document verification

Security & Trust

  • MultiFernet cryptography for sensitive fields
  • One-way hashing for identity de-identification
  • Async malware scanning with ClamAV (python_clamd)
  • Audit-friendly data flows for healthcare environments

Cloud & Networking

  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
  • Cloudflare DNS, WAF, rate limiting & abuse protection
  • Caddy for automatic TLS and reverse proxying
  • Zero-trust posture for internal services

Failure & Abuse Handling

  • Blast containment via storage & permission boundaries
  • Read-only degradation on billing or policy violations
  • Automated cleanup of non-compliant data
  • Systems designed assuming eventual misuse

Bot & Abuse Protection

  • Google reCAPTCHA — Used within Tembo EMR admin surfaces to protect sensitive operational workflows from automated abuse.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — Used on the Clinical Records Hub (CRH) blog and public-facing surfaces as a privacy-preserving, cookie-less bot mitigation layer.
